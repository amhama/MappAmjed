  • MAPPA
  • MAPPA is a Japanese animation studio known for its high-quality work on popular anime series. Founded in 2011, it's recognized for its diverse and visually striking projects, including "Yuri on Ice," "Dorohedoro," and "Jujutsu Kaisen." MAPPA is praised for its attention to detail and innovative animation techniques.
  • Jujutsu Kaisen
  • anime and manga series that follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses after swallowing a powerful cursed object. He joins a group of sorcerers to fight dangerous spirits and protect humanity. Known for its intense battles, unique characters, and deep lore, JJK has gained a massive fanbase.
